You could say that dogs are a man's best friend, which would be a very accurate description of our furry friends. Dogs come in all shapes and sizes. There are little dogs, medium sized dogs, and big dogs. Not only are they a great life companion, but they can do much more than be there for you to love on them. They are used for sport, such as hunting.

Different dogs have different personalities. Just like every person has differing personalities. For example my dog, Duke, is a huge chocolate lab with a wild and crazy personality; however, my friend has a dachshund named Rue, and his personality is much more reserved than Duke's. Dogs that are more playful may be a better fit to use in outdoor activities like hunting or even dog shows. To train dogs for these types of activities can take months, but it is well worth it because your pet will come back much more obedient than he or she was before.

The most rewarding part about having a dog as a pet is the companionship. They are there whenever you need them, and they always approach you with a big, slobbery smile. In good times or even bad times, dogs can be the most comforting part of your day. All of these are reasons why dog, is a man's best friend.
